Recommendations by Marketing Function

Based on the campaign performance analysis, we've identified specific recommendations for each marketing function to optimize the Ramadan 2026 campaign strategy.

Paid Media

  • Continue using PMAX campaigns (contributed 36.82% of total sessions)
  • Maintain investment in premium inventory partners (MEmob, Shahid, Disney+, Sky)
  • Maximize delivery in first half of campaign to avoid competition-driven cost inflation
  • Sustain investment in India, Italy, France, and Germany due to strong performance
  • Adjust bidding strategies during high competition periods to address CPV efficiency

Social Media

  • Prioritize video content over static content, especially for culture-themed messaging
  • Focus on storytelling formats like "Ramadan Diaries" which performed exceptionally well
  • Implement continuous sentiment tracking to ensure content alignment with audience preferences
  • Develop more carousel content featuring Ramadan events and activities
  • Target Italian market with dedicated content (highest engagement with Ramadan storytelling)

PR & Communications

  • Start planning earlier for Ramadan 2026 to secure more media opportunities
  • Create more visual content showcasing unique Ramadan traditions in Abu Dhabi
  • Prioritize FAM trips from France and India (generated highest media value)
  • Use listicle format content which performed well across all markets
  • Coordinate closer with tourism partners to feature their Ramadan offerings

Influencer Marketing

  • Increase influencer collaborations (organic influencer content outperformed standard assets)
  • Prioritize authentic, influencer-led storytelling to drive higher engagement
  • Secure list of Iftar, Suhoor, and other activations well in advance for better planning
  • Continue successful activations (Emirates Palace, Park Hyatt Saadiyat, Al Hosn, Ramadan Arcades)
  • Develop more integrated experiences showcasing both traditional and modern Ramadan

Content Strategy

  • Balance cultural heritage with modern development (strongly appealed to audiences)
  • Create content showcasing Abu Dhabi's destinations and events during Ramadan
  • Produce more content for Iftar section (attracted 4+ minutes average engagement)
  • Develop content highlighting both traditional and modern Ramadan experiences
  • Create content that tells a captivating story about Abu Dhabi as both exciting and inviting

Website & Digital Experience

  • Optimize Suhoor and Iftar sections (outperformed other content areas)
  • Maintain engaging user experience (52.5% engagement rate, 2+ minute session duration)
  • Continue developing content that drives deep engagement
  • Ensure mobile optimization (significant traffic from mobile devices)
  • Improve conversion paths from high-engagement sections

Campaign Planning & Timing

  • Account for Winter Ramadan timing in 2026 (February-March)
  • Begin campaign planning earlier for better cross-functional coordination
  • Consider staggered approach for different markets based on booking windows
  • Plan for potential overlap with other campaigns to avoid audience competition
  • Develop more integrated approach across all marketing functions

Market-Specific Insights

Different markets showed distinct patterns of engagement and response to the Ramadan campaign, providing valuable insights for market-specific strategies in 2026.

GCC Markets

  • Strong performance: 543.1M impressions (68% above forecast)
  • High engagement with authentic religious experiences and charitable activities
  • Shorter booking window (24-30 days) requires later campaign launch
  • Local social media achieved 85% positive sentiment with minimal negative feedback

European Markets

  • France and Italy showed highest potential among European markets
  • Video content showcasing cultural heritage with modern development strongly appealed
  • Longer booking window (61-74 days) requires earlier campaign launch
  • Content showcasing both traditional and modern Ramadan experiences performed well

New Markets

  • India, Italy, France, and Germany consistently delivered highest CTRs and VTRs
  • Strong audience engagement despite being first-time markets for Ramadan campaigns
  • Recommend sustained investment in these markets for 2026

Booking Window Strategy

With Ramadan 2026 occurring February 17 - March 21, campaign launches must be precisely timed based on each market's booking window:

  • Early December 2025: Italy (74 days) & UK (70 days)
  • Mid-December 2025: Germany (62 days) & France (61 days)
  • Mid-January 2026: Qatar (30 days)
  • Late January 2026: KSA (24 days) & UAE (24-28 days)

Key Opportunities for Ramadan 2026

Based on the 2025 campaign performance and the unique timing of Ramadan 2026, several key opportunities have been identified to maximize campaign effectiveness.

Winter Ramadan Timing

Leverage February-March timing with shorter fasting hours (11-12 hours vs. 15-16 hours in summer) and mild temperatures (22-28°C) to create ideal conditions for outdoor Ramadan activities.

Video Storytelling

Expand successful "Ramadan Diaries" format across all markets, as video content significantly outperformed static content, especially for culture-themed messaging.

Influencer Integration

Increase authentic influencer-led content which outperformed standard assets, with Rav Shana's exploring Abu Dhabi reel receiving 6.7K engagements vs. 3.9K on official content.

New Market Expansion

Continue building on strong performance in India, Italy, France, and Germany, which showed strong audience responsiveness despite being first-time markets for Ramadan campaigns.

Premium Partnerships

Maintain investment in premium partners (MEmob, Shahid, Disney+, Sky) which consistently achieved higher quality engagements and incremental reach.

Early Planning

Begin campaign planning earlier to allow for better coordination across all functions, especially securing Iftar, Suhoor, and other activations well in advance.

Strategic Recommendations

Based on the comprehensive analysis of the Ramadan 2025 campaign performance, we've developed strategic recommendations to enhance the effectiveness of the Ramadan 2026 campaign.

Cross-Channel Integration

  • Develop a unified content calendar that coordinates messaging across all channels
  • Create shared asset libraries to ensure visual and messaging consistency
  • Implement cross-channel attribution modeling to better understand the customer journey
  • Coordinate influencer content with paid media flights to maximize impact
  • Ensure PR messaging aligns with and amplifies paid and social content themes

Audience Segmentation

  • Develop market-specific content strategies based on the distinct preferences shown in the data
  • Create separate content streams for GCC audiences versus European audiences
  • Implement more granular targeting parameters based on the engagement data
  • Develop specialized content for the Italian market, which showed highest engagement
  • Create educational content for European markets that addresses misconceptions

Budget Allocation

  • Implement a data-driven budget allocation model based on 2025 performance
  • Shift budget from lower-performing tactics to higher-performing ones
  • Allocate more resources to video content, which significantly outperformed static content
  • Create a flexible budget reserve that can be deployed to capitalize on opportunities
  • Prioritize premium inventory partners that delivered higher quality engagements

Measurement & Optimization

  • Implement continuous sentiment tracking throughout the campaign period
  • Develop a real-time dashboard for cross-channel performance monitoring
  • Create market-specific KPIs that reflect the different objectives for each audience
  • Establish regular optimization checkpoints to adjust tactics based on performance
  • Implement A/B testing for key campaign elements to drive continuous improvement

Competitive Differentiation

  • Conduct competitive analysis before the 2026 campaign to identify unique positioning
  • Develop Abu Dhabi-specific Ramadan experiences that can't be replicated elsewhere
  • Create a distinctive visual and messaging approach that separates Abu Dhabi
  • Highlight Abu Dhabi's unique balance of tradition and modernity during Ramadan
  • Leverage the winter timing of Ramadan 2026 as a competitive advantage

Key Strategic Insight

The most successful elements of the 2025 campaign were those that balanced authentic Ramadan traditions with modern, accessible experiences. This balance should be the cornerstone of the 2026 strategy, with particular emphasis on creating content that educates European markets while resonating with GCC audiences.
