GCC Markets

GCC Markets Overview

GCC markets demonstrate deep understanding of Ramadan (94-100%) with high religious observance. These markets show strong interest in sharing traditions with visitors, particularly Iftar meals, Zakat charity practice, and cultural festivities.

European Markets

European Markets Overview

European markets show significant knowledge gaps about Ramadan (49-60% have limited/no knowledge) but maintain positive associations with its spiritual and cultural aspects. Despite these gaps, there is strong interest in cultural experiences and high consideration for Abu Dhabi as a destination during Ramadan.
