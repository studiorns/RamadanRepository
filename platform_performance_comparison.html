Investment Analysis

This analysis shows the distribution of campaign investment across markets and phases, highlighting regional investment priorities and phase allocation strategies.

Key Investment Insights: The campaign investment was strategically allocated across 12 markets, with European markets receiving the largest share (62.2%), followed by GCC markets (28.8%), India (6.9%), and Armenia (0.3%). Platform Investment Analysis

This analysis shows the distribution of campaign investment across platforms and phases, highlighting platform specialization and strategic allocation patterns.

Key Platform Insights: META (34.2%) and YouTube (27.3%) received the largest share of the campaign budget, followed by PMAX (10.4%) and Sky (7.4%). Key Strategic Insights

  • Platform Specialization: Each platform demonstrated distinct strengths - META for cost-efficient reach, Snap for sessions, YouTube for quality views, and PMAX for traffic.
  • Market-Platform Alignment: Performance varied significantly by market, with Snap excelling in Saudi Arabia, YouTube in Germany, and META in the UK.
  • Phased Approach Effectiveness: The two-phase strategy (Awareness → Traffic) was effective but could be optimized with better platform allocation.
  • Premium vs. Efficiency Trade-off: Premium platforms (Sky, Disney+, Shahid) delivered exceptional view quality but at significantly higher costs.

Core Recommendations

  • Optimize Platform Mix: Increase Snap's budget share (15-20%), maintain META (35-40%) and YouTube (25-30%), and reduce TikTok (0-5%).
  • Implement Market-Specific Strategies: Develop tailored platform approaches for key markets based on performance data.
  • Enhance Creative Approach: Tailor creative assets to each platform's strengths and audience behaviors.
  • Expand Phase Testing: Test Snap in the Traffic phase given its strong performance in generating cost-efficient sessions.
  • Establish Real-time Optimization: Implement continuous performance monitoring to quickly adjust strategies based on platform performance.

Market Efficiency Score (MES) Methodology

The Market Efficiency Score (MES) methodology provides a standardized approach for evaluating market performance across both Awareness and Traffic phases. This calculation accounts for the distinct objectives of each campaign phase, allowing for consistent comparison across markets with different investment levels and performance patterns.

Phase-Specific MES Formulas:

  • Awareness Phase MES = (0.4 × Impression Efficiency) + (0.4 × View Efficiency) + (0.1 × Click Efficiency) + (0.1 × Quality Factor)
  • Traffic Phase MES = (0.1 × Impression Efficiency) + (0.5 × Click Efficiency) + (0.3 × Session Efficiency) + (0.1 × Quality Factor)
  • Overall Standardized MES = (0.7 × Awareness Phase MES) + (0.3 × Traffic Phase MES)

This approach ensures that markets are evaluated based on metrics that align with campaign objectives, with impression and view efficiency weighted more heavily in the Awareness phase, while the Traffic phase prioritizes click and session efficiency.

Conclusion & Strategic Recommendations

The Ramadan campaign utilized multiple platforms with a total investment of $4.04 million, generating 1.93 billion impressions, 305.4 million views, 8.96 million clicks, and 1.28 million sessions. The campaign effectively employed a multi-platform strategy with distinct phases for awareness and traffic generation.

Key Strategic Insights

  • Platform Specialization: Each platform demonstrated distinct strengths that should inform future media allocation:
    • META: Excellent for cost-efficient reach, views, and versatile campaign objectives
    • Snap: Superior for generating high-quality traffic and sessions at efficient costs
    • YouTube: Exceptional for high-quality video views and engagement
    • TikTok: Underperformed across most metrics, requiring significant optimization
  • Market-Platform Alignment: Performance varied significantly by market, suggesting opportunities for market-specific platform strategies:
    • Middle Eastern Markets: Snap performed exceptionally well in Saudi Arabia; YouTube delivered strong view rates in UAE
    • European Markets: META delivered strong session performance in UK; YouTube achieved exceptional view rates in Germany
  • Campaign Phasing Strategy: The two-phase approach (Awareness → Traffic) was effective, but platform utilization could be optimized:
    • Awareness Phase: Effective multi-platform approach with each platform contributing to different objectives
    • Traffic Phase: Over-reliance on META; opportunity to test Snap given its strong traffic efficiency
